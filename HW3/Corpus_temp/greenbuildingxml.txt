<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p>The <b>Green Building XML</b> schema (<b>gbXML</b>) is an <a href="/wiki/Open_standard" title="Open standard">open</a> <a href="/wiki/XML_schema" title="XML schema">schema</a> developed to facilitate transfer of building data stored in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Building_Information_Model" title="Building Information Model">Building Information Models</a> (BIM) to engineering analysis tools. gbXML is being integrated into a range of software CAD and engineering tools and supported by leading 3D BIM vendors. gbXML is streamlined to transfer building properties to and from engineering analysis tools to reduce the interoperability issues and eliminate plan take-off time.</p>
<p>gbXML is the underlying architecture of <a href="/wiki/Autodesk" title="Autodesk">Autodesk</a>'s Green Building Studio commercial on-line energy analysis product,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and is the main export option for energy analysis from their modeling products.</p>
