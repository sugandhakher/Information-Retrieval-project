<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p><b>Sustainable House Day</b> is a national event held annually across <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>, where <a href="/wiki/Household" title="Household">households</a> open their doors to the public to give interested people the chance to learn about the benefits of <a href="/wiki/Efficient_energy_use" title="Efficient energy use">energy efficiency</a> in their <a href="/wiki/Home" title="Home">homes</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-weeklytimesnow.com.au_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-weeklytimesnow.com.au-1">[1]</a></sup> People have the opportunity to talk with sustainable home owners, <a href="/wiki/Architect" title="Architect">architects</a>, <a href="/wiki/Construction_worker" title="Construction worker">builders</a>, <a href="/wiki/Designer" title="Designer">designers</a> and energy auditors to learn about <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_living" title="Sustainable living">sustainable living</a>. The 2016 date is September 11.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
