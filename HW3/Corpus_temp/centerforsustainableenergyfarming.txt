<p>The <b>Center for Sustainable Energy Farming,</b> established in late 2010 by <a href="/wiki/Global_Clean_Energy_Holdings" title="Global Clean Energy Holdings">Global Clean Energy Holdings</a>, is a non-profit research center dedicated to establishing and promoting the development of resilient and sustainable communities, fueled by socially responsible clean energy produced from <a href="/wiki/Jatropha" title="Jatropha">Jatropha</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Mission">Mission</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Center_for_Sustainable_Energy_Farming&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Mission">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Center‘s mission is to perform plant science research in <a href="/wiki/Genetics" title="Genetics">genetics</a>, <a href="/wiki/Plant_breeding" title="Plant breeding">breeding</a> and <a href="/wiki/Horticulture" title="Horticulture">horticulture</a>, and further develop technologies to allow for the economic commercialization and sustainability of energy farms. The Center provides a common research platform to foster communication and cooperation among various national and international research institutions and organizations in the biofuel sector. From a global economics, environmental and social perspective, this collaboration will contribute to the development of more productive energy farms capable of supplying large quantities of plant-based (non-food) oils to replace the use of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fossil_fuels" title="Fossil fuels">fossil fuels</a>. This initiative has the potential to create tens of thousands of jobs in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Agri-business" title="Agri-business">agri-business</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alternative_energy" title="Alternative energy">alternative energy</a> industries, expand the use of non-productive land, generate local business-to-business opportunities, reduce global dependency on oil reserves, and contribute to the reduction of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Carbon_emissions" title="Carbon emissions">carbon emissions</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Current_research">Current research</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Center_for_Sustainable_Energy_Farming&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Current research">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Center is focused on developing high-yielding commercial varieties of <a href="/wiki/Jatropha_curcas" title="Jatropha curcas">Jatropha curcas</a> through interdisciplinary research cooperation across multiple scientific disciplines.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The center has a goal of improving yields 300 percent and reducing inputs by 30 percent within 10 years.</p>
