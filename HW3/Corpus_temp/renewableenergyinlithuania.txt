<p>In 2010 <b>Renewable energy in Lithuania</b> constituted 19.7% of the country's overall electricity generation.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The Lithuanian government aims to generate 23% of total power from renewable resources by 2020.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biomass">Biomass</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Renewable_energy_in_Lithuania&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Biomass">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Biomass_in_Lithuania" title="Biomass in Lithuania">Biomass in Lithuania</a></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Biomass" title="Biomass">Biomass</a> represents the most common source of renewable energy in Lithuania, with most of the biomass is use being <a href="/wiki/Firewood" title="Firewood">firewood</a>. The amount of energy generated from biomass in Lithuania is the second highest in the EU per capita. It is estimated that in 2020 the country will lead the EU in the quantity of biomass available for biofuel production.</p>

<div class="hatnote" role="note">Main article: <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Hydroelectric_power_in_Lithuania&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Hydroelectric power in Lithuania (page does not exist)">Hydroelectric power in Lithuania</a></div>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kruonis_Pumped_Storage_Plant" title="Kruonis Pumped Storage Plant">Kruonis Pumped Storage Plant</a>, its main purpose is to provide a spinning reserve of the power system, to regulate the load curve of the power system 24 hours a day. Installed capacity of the pumped storage plant: 900 MW (4 units, 225 MW each).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kaunas_Hydroelectric_Power_Plant" title="Kaunas Hydroelectric Power Plant">Kaunas Hydroelectric Power Plant</a>, it supplies about 3% of the electrical demand in Lithuania.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-kh_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-kh-5">[5]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
