<p><b>Silesian University of Technology</b> (<a href="/wiki/Polish_language" title="Polish language">Polish</a> name: <b>Politechnika Śląska</b> IPA: [pɔliˈtɛxɲika ˈɕlɔ̃ska]) is a <a href="/wiki/University" title="University">university</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Gliwice" title="Gliwice">Gliwice</a>, <a href="/wiki/Silesia" title="Silesia">Silesia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Poland</a>. It was founded in 1945 by Polish professors of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lviv_University_of_Technology" title="Lviv University of Technology">Lwow Polytechnic</a>, who were forced to leave their native city and move to the <a href="/wiki/Recovered_Territories" title="Recovered Territories">Recovered Territories</a> (see also <a href="/wiki/Kresy" title="Kresy">Kresy</a>). The University consistently ranks among the top most prestigious technical universities in Poland.</p>
<p>Silesian University of Technology is divided into 13 faculties, 2 colleges, 1 Institute and 2 Science and Education Centres:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Architecture" title="Architecture">Architecture</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Automatic_control" title="Automatic control">Automatic control</a>, <a href="/wiki/Electronics" title="Electronics">Electronics</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Computer_Science" title="Computer Science">Computer Science</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Civil_Engineering" title="Civil Engineering">Civil Engineering</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Chemistry" title="Chemistry">Chemistry</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Electrical_Engineering" title="Electrical Engineering">Electrical Engineering</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Mining" title="Mining">Mining</a> and <a href="/wiki/Geology" title="Geology">Geology</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Biomedical_Engineering" title="Biomedical Engineering">Biomedical Engineering</a></li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Materials_science" title="Materials science">Materials Engineering</a> and <a href="/wiki/Metallurgy" title="Metallurgy">Metallurgy</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Energy" title="Energy">Energy</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Environmental_Engineering" title="Environmental Engineering">Environmental Engineering</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Applied_Mathematics" title="Applied Mathematics">Applied Mathematics</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mechanical_Engineering" title="Mechanical Engineering">Mechanical Engineering</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Organizing_(management)" title="Organizing (management)">Organization</a> and <a href="/wiki/Management" title="Management">Management</a>,</li>
<li>Faculty of <a href="/wiki/Transport" title="Transport">Transport</a>,</li>
<li>College of <a href="/wiki/Philology" title="Philology">Foreign Languages</a>,</li>
<li>College of <a href="/wiki/Pedagogy" title="Pedagogy">Education</a>,</li>
<li>Institute of <a href="/wiki/Physics" title="Physics">Physics</a> - Centre for Science and Education,</li>
<li>Centre of Science and Education - <a href="/wiki/Engineering" title="Engineering">Engineering</a> Education Centre,</li>
<li>Centre of Science and Education for <a href="/wiki/Rail_transport" title="Rail transport">Railway Transportation</a>.</li>
</ul>
<p>Twelve units (faculties, colleges and institute) are situated in <a href="/wiki/Gliwice" title="Gliwice">Gliwice</a>, two in <a href="/wiki/Katowice" title="Katowice">Katowice</a> and two in <a href="/wiki/Zabrze" title="Zabrze">Zabrze</a>. Centres of science and education are located in <a href="/wiki/Rybnik" title="Rybnik">Rybnik</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sosnowiec" title="Sosnowiec">Sosnowiec</a>.</p>
