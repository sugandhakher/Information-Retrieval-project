<p>The <b>Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority</b> (or <b>SLSEA</b>) is the primary body responsible for the issuance of licenses to <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_energy" title="Sustainable energy">sustainable energy</a> developments in <a href="/wiki/Sri_Lanka" title="Sri Lanka">Sri Lanka</a>. In addition to being the key licence provider, it is also in charge of promoting <a href="/wiki/Renewable_energy" title="Renewable energy">renewable energy</a> and sustainable developments in the country.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
