<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p>The <b>World Energy Engineering Congress</b> (WEEC) is an international energy industry conference and exposition hosted annually by the <a href="/wiki/Association_of_Energy_Engineers" title="Association of Energy Engineers">Association of Energy Engineers</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Professionals in the field of <a href="/wiki/Energy_engineering" title="Energy engineering">energy engineering</a> from around the world convene annually at the WEEC to discuss energy-related issues and technology such as:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Efficient_energy_use" title="Efficient energy use">Energy efficiency</a> and <a href="/wiki/Energy_management" title="Energy management">energy management</a></li>
<li>Renewable, green, and alternative energy</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Combined_heat_and_power" title="Combined heat and power">Combined heat and power</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cogeneration" title="Cogeneration">cogeneration</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Distributed_generation" title="Distributed generation">distributed generation</a></li>
<li>Integrated <a href="/wiki/Building_automation" title="Building automation">building automation</a> and energy management</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lighting_efficiency" title="Lighting efficiency">Lighting efficiency</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/HVAC" title="HVAC">HVAC</a> systems and controls</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Thermal_storage" title="Thermal storage">Thermal storage</a> and <a href="/wiki/Load_management" title="Load management">load management</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Boilers" title="Boilers">Boilers</a> and combustion controls</li>
<li>Geoexchange technologies</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Solar_energy" title="Solar energy">Solar</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fuel_cell" title="Fuel cell">fuel cell</a> technologies</li>
<li>Applications specific to federal emergency management programs</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Energy_services" title="Energy services">Energy services</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Project_financing" title="Project financing">project financing</a></li>
</ul>
