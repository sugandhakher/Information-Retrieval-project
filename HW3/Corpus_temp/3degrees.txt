<p><b>3Degrees</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Benefit_corporation" title="Benefit corporation">B corporation</a> with offices across the United States that provides <a href="/wiki/Renewable_energy" title="Renewable energy">renewable energy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Carbon_offset" title="Carbon offset">carbon offset</a> solutions to <a href="/wiki/Fortune_500" title="Fortune 500">Fortune 500</a> companies, utilities, universities, green building firms and other organizations that are working to make their services and operations more sustainable.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/U.S._Environmental_Protection_Agency" title="U.S. Environmental Protection Agency">U.S. Environmental Protection Agency</a> has recognized 3Degrees as an eight-time winner of the Green Power Supplier of the Year award while Environmental Finance Magazine has named the company #1 REC Trading Company in North America five times.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Services">Services</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=3Degrees&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Services">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>3Degrees offers the following renewable energy services:</p>
<ul>
<li>Renewable Energy Procurement: working with organizations to develop <a href="/wiki/Power_purchase_agreement" title="Power purchase agreement">power purchase agreements</a> and custom renewable energy strategies and implementation plans.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:1_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-:1-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li>Community Solar: cinsulting with utilities who are looking for turnkey <a href="/wiki/Community_solar_farm" title="Community solar farm">community solar</a> programs and solar developers.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:2_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-:2-3">[3]</a></sup> 3Degrees helps with issues such as: facility sizing and location, REC and facility ownership, customer acquisition and retention, billing integration and risk mitigation.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:2_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-:2-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li>Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs): procuring <a href="/wiki/Renewable_Energy_Certificate_(United_States)" title="Renewable Energy Certificate (United States)">RECs</a> that are Green-e Energy certified and/or eligible for compliance with state <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Renewable_Portfolio_Standards" title="Renewable Portfolio Standards">Renewable Portfolio Standards</a> (RPS).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:3_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-:3-4">[4]</a></sup></li>
<li>LEED: partnering with green building professionals to help them reduce the environmental impact of the energy use associated with their buildings, allowing them to earn points toward the U.S. Green Building Council’s (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/USGBC" title="USGBC">USGBC</a>) <a href="/wiki/Leadership_in_Energy_and_Environmental_Design" title="Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design">Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design</a> (LEED) certification.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:3_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-:3-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:4_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-:4-5">[5]</a></sup></li>
<li>Green Power Programs: working with utilities to design voluntary green power programs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:5_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-:5-6">[6]</a></sup></li>
<li>Carbon Offsets: sourcing <a href="/wiki/Carbon_offset" title="Carbon offset">carbon offsets</a> which are verified.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:6_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-:6-7">[7]</a></sup> Common carbon reduction project technologies include: <a href="/wiki/Solar_energy" title="Solar energy">solar</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wind_power" title="Wind power">wind</a>, <a href="/wiki/Landfill_gas" title="Landfill gas">landfill gas</a>, livestock <a href="/wiki/Biogas" title="Biogas">biogas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Biomass" title="Biomass">biomass</a>, low-impact hydro and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sustainable_forestry" title="Sustainable forestry">sustainable forestry</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:6_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-:6-7">[7]</a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Associations_.26_Memberships">Associations &amp; Memberships</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=3Degrees&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Associations &amp; Memberships">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>3Degrees is a member of the <a href="/wiki/U.S._Green_Building_Council" title="U.S. Green Building Council">U.S. Green Building Council</a> (USGBC),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:4_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-:4-5">[5]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/The_Climate_Registry" title="The Climate Registry">The Climate Registry</a> (CR),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:8_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-:8-8">[8]</a></sup> Business Renewables Center (BRC),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:9_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-:9-9">[9]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Solar_Energy_Industries_Association" title="Solar Energy Industries Association">Solar Energy Industries Association</a> (SEIA)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:10_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-:10-10">[10]</a></sup> and Smart Electric Power Alliance (SEPA).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:11_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-:11-11">[11]</a></sup></p>
