<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p><b>Energy-efficient landscaping</b> is a type of <a href="/wiki/Landscape_architecture" title="Landscape architecture">landscaping</a> designed for the purpose of <a href="/wiki/Energy_conservation" title="Energy conservation">conserving energy</a>. There is a distinction between the embedded energy of materials and constructing the landscape, and the energy consumed by the maintenance and operations of a landscape.</p>
<p>Design techniques include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Planting <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Trees" title="Trees">trees</a> for the purpose of providing <a href="/wiki/Shade_(shadow)" title="Shade (shadow)">shade</a>, which reduces cooling costs.</li>
<li>Planting or building <a href="/wiki/Windbreak" title="Windbreak">windbreaks</a> to slow winds near buildings, which reduces heat loss.</li>
<li>Wall sheltering, where <a href="/wiki/Shrubbery" title="Shrubbery">shrubbery</a> or vines are used to create a windbreak directly against a wall.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Earth_sheltering" title="Earth sheltering">Earth sheltering</a> and positioning buildings to take advantage of natural landforms as windbreaks.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Green_roof" title="Green roof">Green roofs</a> that cool buildings with extra <a href="/wiki/Thermal_mass" title="Thermal mass">thermal mass</a> and <a href="/wiki/Evapotranspiration" title="Evapotranspiration">evapotranspiration</a>.</li>
<li>Reducing the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Heat_island_effect" title="Heat island effect">heat island effect</a> with pervious paving, high <a href="/wiki/Albedo" title="Albedo">albedo</a> paving, shade, and minimizing paved areas.</li>
<li>Site lighting with full cut off fixtures, light level sensors, and high efficiency fixtures</li>
</ul>
<p>Energy-efficient landscaping techniques include using local materials, on-site composting and chipping to reduce <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Greenwaste&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Greenwaste (page does not exist)">greenwaste</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hauling" title="Hauling">hauling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hand_tool" title="Hand tool">hand tools</a> instead of gasoline-powered, and also may involve using <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drought-resistant" title="Drought-resistant">drought-resistant</a> plantings in <a href="/wiki/Arid" title="Arid">arid</a> areas, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Buy_local" title="Buy local">buying stock from local growers</a> to avoid energy in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Transportation" title="Transportation">transportation</a>, and similar techniques.</p>
