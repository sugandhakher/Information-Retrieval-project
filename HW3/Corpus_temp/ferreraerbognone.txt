<p><b>Ferrera Erbognone</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Pavia" title="Province of Pavia">Province of Pavia</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Lombardy" title="Lombardy">Lombardy</a>, located about 45 km southwest of <a href="/wiki/Milan" title="Milan">Milan</a> and about 25 km southwest of <a href="/wiki/Pavia" title="Pavia">Pavia</a>. It is included in the lower <a href="/wiki/Lomellina" title="Lomellina">Lomellina</a> historical region, on the banks of the <a href="/wiki/Erbognone" title="Erbognone">Erbognone</a> stream, an affluent of the <a href="/wiki/Agogna" title="Agogna">Agogna</a>.</p>
<p>Ferrera Erbognone borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Galliavola" title="Galliavola">Galliavola</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lomello" title="Lomello">Lomello</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mezzana_Bigli" title="Mezzana Bigli">Mezzana Bigli</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ottobiano" title="Ottobiano">Ottobiano</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pieve_del_Cairo" title="Pieve del Cairo">Pieve del Cairo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sannazzaro_de%27_Burgondi" title="Sannazzaro de' Burgondi">Sannazzaro de' Burgondi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scaldasole" title="Scaldasole">Scaldasole</a>, <a href="/wiki/Valeggio,_Lombardy" title="Valeggio, Lombardy">Valeggio</a>.</p>
