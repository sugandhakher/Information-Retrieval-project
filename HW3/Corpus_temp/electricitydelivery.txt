<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><p><b>Electricity delivery</b> is the process that goes from <a href="/wiki/Electricity_generation" title="Electricity generation">generation of electricity</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Power_station" title="Power station">power station</a> to the use by the <a href="/wiki/Consumer" title="Consumer">consumer</a>.</p>
<p>The main processes in electricity delivery are, by order:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Electricity_generation" title="Electricity generation">Generation</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Electricity_transmission" title="Electricity transmission">Transmission</a></li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Electricity_distribution" title="Electricity distribution">Distribution</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Electricity_retailing" title="Electricity retailing">Retailing</a></li>
</ul>
