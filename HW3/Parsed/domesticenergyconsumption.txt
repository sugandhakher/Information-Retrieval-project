domestic
or domestic consumption of energy is the total amount of energy used in a house for household work  the amount of energy used per household varies widely depending on the standard of living of the country  the climate  and the age and type of residence 
in the united states as of 2008  in an average household in a temperate climate  the yearly use of household energy can be composed as follows
