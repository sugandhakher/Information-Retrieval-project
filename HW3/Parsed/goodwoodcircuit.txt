goodwood festival of speed  
main article 
the goodwood festival of speed is an annual hill climb  held in late june or early july not on the circuit  but in the nearby grounds of goodwood house  it features historic and modern motor-racing vehicles  in 2010  the event had over 176,000 visitors over four days 
goodwood revival  
main article 
following the success of the festival of speed hill climb  racing returned to the goodwood circuit in 1998  the goodwood revival is a three-day festival held each september for the types of cars and motorcycles that would have competed during the circuit s original period  19481966  historic aircraft help to complete the vintage feel  in 2008  a crowd of 68,000 people attended the event on the main sunday - 9,000 more than in 2007    the track is now used for classic races  track days  and try-out days  nearly everyone dresses up in vintage outfit from mods and rockers to racing drivers and just smart period clothes
