the european biomass association  aebiom  from the official french name association europenne pour la biomasse  is a european trade association opens to national biomass associations and bioenergy companies active in europe  aebiom was founded in 1990 and aims to promote biomass production and application throughout europe 
aebiom is the umbrella organisation of the european pellet council  epc   1  the european industry of pellet suppliers  eips  and the international biomass torrefaction council  ibtc   2
