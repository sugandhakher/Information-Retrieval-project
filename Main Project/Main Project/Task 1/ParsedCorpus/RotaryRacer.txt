rotary racer is a greenpower team which started in 2005 as an initiative within chipping sodbury school to try to get  lads and dads  working together on a project to build and race their own electric racing cars  ever since the team has grown  with 9 different cars being built within the past 9 years  the team are made up of both girls and boys  ranging between the ages of 11 and 18

the name rotary racer came from the team s first sponsors -rotary international of chipping sodbury  this charity provided the funding needed to start the car from scratch and  ever since  have continued to sponsor the team each new season  over the years  even more companies have sponsored the team so they are able to continue racing as well as improve their car  the current sponsors are
